Peanut Butter Cookies

An example of what this delicious recipe will create

In this recipe you will follow along creating delicious peanut butter cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 a cup of chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda

Directions

  1. Set oven to 350 F
  2. Mix ingredients
  3. Form into palm sized balls
  4. Place onto baking sheet 2 inches apart
  5. Place in oven and let bake for 8-10 minutes
